The Dexterous core is far enough ahead of the Magic (Non-Magic Variant) core that it's the best one to use for Hitch. I do not recommend even trying the Magic core because it doesn't have any health nodes.

Note that the damage of the Dexterous core varies depending on the number of 15+ Dexterity champions in the formation. The damage numbers on the layout above account for just 1 (Hitch only). For a specific breakdown of how the core fares with more 15+ Dexterity champions - check the table below.
