The Magic (Non-Magic Variant) core is far enough ahead of the other cores that it's the best one to use for Vi. That said - you could potentially use the Strong (Non-Melee Variant) or Modest core if the pipes you have favours one of them. Though it is worth noting that the Magic core doesn't have any health nodes - which could influence your choice.
